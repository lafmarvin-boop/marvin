Drop your custom Picovoice Porcupine wake-word file here, named:

    yo_poto_android.ppn

Steps:
1. Sign up free at https://console.picovoice.ai/
2. "Porcupine" → "Train a wake word" → enter "Yo Poto", platform Android (arm64).
3. Download the .ppn and rename it to yo_poto_android.ppn.
4. Move it next to this README.
5. Add your AccessKey to local.properties at the project root:
       PICOVOICE_ACCESS_KEY=xxxxxxxx
